FORMA Printing Group

Forma Printing Group has been in existence since 1996. It was primarily engaged in magazine production until 2012, when it acquired a new owner while retaining the same name. With a strong desire for innovation and a love for designing cardboard and paper products, the production shifted towards creating a wide range of packaging products and other cardboard items, as well as products made from thinner paper. Equipped with modern machines, efficient organization, and a well-coordinated team, they achieve maximum quality, delivery speed, and competitive pricing.

Start Page

The Start Page is ones of my personal projects which is supposed to replace your default start/home page in the browser with a modern and minimalistic one. The one when you open your browser or when you open a new tab inside of it. I wanted to make it because I didn't quite like the visual design of pretty much any start page of the most commonly used modern web browsers. Most of the basics are already implemented, but over time I plan on adding more features to it and refining the design.

BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator is one of my personal projects which aims to create an interactive and user-friendly web application that allows users to calculate their Body Mass Index (BMI) quickly and easily. BMI is a simple yet essential measure of a person's overall health based on their height and weight. It is available only in metric measuring system for now. I am planning or refreshing it's overall design and functionality soon since this is a 2 year old project and I feel like I can do much better nowadays. I already have the mockups ready.

Learn Serbian alpha

Learn Serbian is a web application project I started working on by myself in 2022 and its purpose is to help people learn the Serbian language. As it got more complex my friend drB0r1s joined me and focused mostly on the development of the website while I sticked to the design. It is still in early stages and is only desktop friendly at the moment. It does not have any backend at all. Recently we slowed down quite a lot because we’ve both been quite busy. But we hope we will eventually have enough free time to finish it.
